Some Useful Insights From This Dataset

  • In Scandinavian countries, wind power is the most common source of fuel.
  • In Thailand, wind power is the most common source of fuel.
  • United States ha the maximum number of power plants at over 8119, this contrasts interestingly with China (2901) and India (845) which have much larger populations to support. In fact, France with its relatively small population has over 2000 power plants
  • Middle Eastern countries largely rely on gas and oil as expected due to their large reserves.
  • The number of wind and solar plants is greater than the number of oil, gas and coal plants. This signals a clear change in policy around the world.
